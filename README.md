

# **TechPathfinder**
<img width="1860" height="730" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/60107931-9753-4e73-ba82-014bfed90f4d" />


**TechPathfinder** is a website built using **Lovable.ai** to help tech graduates (CS, IT, DS, AI, SE, B.Tech) discover **global opportunities**, including scholarships, internships, jobs, fellowships, hackathons, and open-source projects. It also provides **personalized career guidance** and preparation roadmaps.

---

## **Project Overview**

Many tech graduates face challenges after graduation:

* Lack of information about global opportunities based on CGPA, skills, and interests.
* High CGPA students often get the spotlight while low-CGPA students are overlooked.
* Graduates spend money on post-graduation without exploring available alternatives.

**TechPathfinder** solves this problem by providing:

1. Scholarship recommendations worldwide.
2. Industry opportunities like jobs, internships, and hackathons.
3. Personalized preparation roadmap for skill improvement.
4. AI-driven career guidance chatbot with realistic career paths.

---

## **Features**

* **User Input Panel:** CGPA, skills, resume, and educational background.
* **Scholarship Finder:** Matches opportunities for high and low CGPA students.
* **Industry Opportunities:** Suggests jobs, internships, hackathons, and open-source events globally.
* **Preparation Roadmap:** Provides learning resources and step-by-step plans for skill improvement.
* **AI Career Advisor Chatbot:** Guides users with examples of successful individuals with similar backgrounds.
* **Modern UI:** Organized, Gemini-inspired design with gradients, glassmorphism, and responsive layout.

---

## **User Journey**

1. User opens TechPathfinder website.
2. Uploads CGPA, resume, and enters skills/interests.
3. Website suggests scholarships or industry opportunities.
4. Generates skill-building roadmap if needed.
5. Interacts with AI Career Advisor.
6. Receives a realistic career path and applies for opportunities or starts preparation.

---

## **Technology Used**

* **Platform:** Lovable.ai (No coding required)
* **Design:** Gemini-inspired color scheme, clean UI, responsive layout
* **AI Features:** Built-in Lovable AI for chatbot and recommendation system

---

## **How to Use**

1. Visit the live website URL.
2. Enter your CGPA, skills, experience, and upload your resume.
3. Explore recommended scholarships, internships, jobs, and hackathons.
4. Use the AI Career Advisor to generate your personalized career path.
5. Follow preparation roadmap and start applying for opportunities.
